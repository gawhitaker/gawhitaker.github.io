Dr Gavin Whitaker

KTP associate at University College London working with Stratagem

Research Interests

  • Bayesian Statistics.
  • Stochastic differential mixed-effects models.
  • Inference using stochastic differential equations (SDEs) and extensions to random effects SDE models.
  • Bridging strategies for SDEs.
  • Markov chain Monte Carlo.
  • The linear noise approximation.
  • Sports modelling.
